Hi, thanks for developing grcov!

On https://doc.wikimedia.org we have a pretty restrictive CSP that only allows local/self-hosted resources for both privacy and security reasons (e.g. [a broken report](https://doc.wikimedia.org/cover/mwbot-rs/mwbot/coverage/)).
