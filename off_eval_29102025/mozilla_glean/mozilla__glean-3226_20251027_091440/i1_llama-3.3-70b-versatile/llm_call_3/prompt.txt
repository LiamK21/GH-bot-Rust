Issue:
<issue>
Pings following another ping's schedule don't get submitted if said ping is disabled
Pings can follow another ping's schedule by annotating them with `metadata.ping_schedule`.
However, if the ping listed in that schedule is submitted, but disabled at submission time, any pings following its schedule won't even be tried.
</issue>
